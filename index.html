About me

I'm a Game Developer

Hi, I’m Lasse Jaara, a game developer who loves creating enjoyable and engaging games for players. I usually go online under the name Jupresson.

I discovered my passion for game creation as a child, making custom maps with my brother on the PS3 in games like Minecraft and LittleBigPlanet Karting. This interest grew into PC gaming, where I began developing and modifying mods, plugins, and servers for Minecraft Java Edition.

Over time, I shifted my focus to building full games, experimenting with Unity and Unreal Engine. My technologies advanced significantly after enrolling in OSAO Kaukovainio Technical School for Game Programming and IT, where I gained deeper knowledge of programming and game development and completed several projects.
